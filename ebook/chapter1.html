The Invitation

Once upon a time in a faraway land in eastern Europe, there was a small village in the borders between Spain and Portugal. This town was closed to the black hills forest a place where people said was enchanted. The elders in the town tell all kind of stories to the children all around. Biona’s town is cold and foggy it gets dark early most of the time. School hours was the only time where you can find people in the streets, pretty much is not a social town for so many reasons. Very soon after the first world war started, the children of the city begin to disappear. Last year it was reported that one hundred and fifty children were missing. It has been happening every year, almost twelve to fourteen kids disappear from home nearly every month and some police reports said this is repeatedly happening for the past years.

With the passing of the year's people move in and move out to this village, and all these stories kept like just that! Tales. It is an unfortunate community in the mountains, all the jobs are in the big city “Sagres” Eight hours away in bus. Most of the time the parents are absent from home during big part of the week. This town used to have happy and friendly people, the local school of this community now is as empty as the rest of the city. Some of the last children in this sad and lonely place are walking to the school. After the death of his mother in a car accident, Charlie's father decided to make a change in their lives, and they moved to start again with Charlie. It was a significant change for him, he would have to leave his friends and his neighborhood that reminded him so much of his mother. After they move to his father hometown Charlie tried to make new friends, one of his new pals is Gonta an eleven years old boy who lives with his grandmother.
