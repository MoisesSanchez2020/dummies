That night was cold and foggy, and Charlie was waiting for Gonta to leave his house to this incredible adventure. They are the first to arrive at the Lonely bridge. "Don't you remember that my brother is there already," Gonta said. "True!" Charlie said. "It's almost midnight!"

"Did you hear that?" Gonta asked. "What!" Charlie asked. "Wait that sound is! Like a circus!" "I love it!" Gonta said. All the children were shaking in the freezing night, but once they all heard the music, they just forgot. Charlie is Mr. toy! wooow!" Gonta said. "School bus!!! Hahaha, that is fantastic!" Charlie Said. Mr. Toy is how they call him, opened the bus door and barely said: "welcome aboard." All the children ran to get the best seat. When everyone was inside, and they sat.
