DUMMIES

Charlie decides to see how big that place was, so he gets into the biggest Ferris wheels to see better. He was able to look at the walls, and was amazed at how big that place was. When he saw the strange looks of the white building in the back, he started to pay more attention to it, even a large parking lot for some large trucks is next to the warehouse. Other trucks left the area, although Charlie thought they could take the children to the hospital, there were no ambulances, only loading trucks. Charlie did not like it, the children still enter the park and get in the white building, but no one comes back to the park. While Charlie was on the ferry wheels thinking, he saw some kids drinking the green sodas. But something strange happens after the children drink the green soft drink.

They all begin acting differently, they cannot stop drinking, they were dizzy and slippery, twenty minutes later they fall to the ground. At all the attractions, in the food court, and the park there were always dummies standing up as if they were controlling everything. When the children fell, a squad of dummies immediately picked them up and took them to the white building in the back. It happens almost every hour, and Charlie decides to sneak out to see what's going on and see where they take the children. So he got off the Ferris wheel, went to the food court and pretended he was drinking the green soda. Charlie starts walking as if he is falling and shaking until he falls. After a few minutes, a squad of dummies went to look for Charlie. He was nervous, but soon the dummies picked him up. He closed his eyes until they were in front of the door of the white building. They all enter a large hall with a light at the end, and there was a large room with a large table with all kinds of dummies. They put Charlie next to other dummies, and they were like in the waiting room. The dummies squad throws Charlie, and they returned to the park.
